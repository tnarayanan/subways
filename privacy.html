Privacy Policy

This privacy policy covers what information is collected by the "Subways" app created by Tejas Narayanan. It is effective as of January 1, 2025, and reflects Subways v1.0.0.

Subways does not collect any information about users. Specifically, users do not create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

Subways stores users' favorite stations and most-recently-viewed station on-device. This information is not used for analytics purposes.

Subways makes data requests to the Metropolitan Transportation Authority (MTA) API (linked here) to get real-time subway arrival data. These requests do not supply the MTA or any other party with any information about the user.

In summary, Subways does not collect any user information! It is designed to be as simple and privacy-conscious as possible, meaning that no user data leaves users' devices.
